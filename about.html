Our Story

A Legacy of Compassionate Care

ANGELS ADULT DAYCARE SERVICES LLC was founded with a singular vision: to create a safe, nurturing environment where adults can receive the care and attention they deserve while maintaining their dignity and independence. Located in the heart of Meridian, Mississippi, we have become a trusted partner for families seeking quality daytime care for their loved ones.

Our journey began when our founders recognized a critical need in the community for professional adult daycare services that go beyond basic supervision. We envisioned a place where participants could engage in meaningful activities, form genuine connections, and experience each day with purpose and joy.

Today, we continue to uphold the values that inspired our founding: compassion, respect, professionalism, and an unwavering commitment to the wellbeing of every individual in our care. Our dedicated team works tirelessly to ensure that each participant feels valued, supported, and part of our extended family.

What Sets Us Apart

Why Families Choose Angels

01

Personalized Care Plans

Every participant receives an individualized care plan tailored to their unique needs, preferences, and health requirements. We work closely with families and healthcare providers to ensure comprehensive, coordinated care.

02

Engaging Daily Programs

Our activity coordinators design stimulating programs that promote cognitive engagement, physical wellness, and social interaction. From arts and crafts to music therapy and gentle exercise, every day brings new opportunities for joy.

03

Professional Staff

Our team consists of certified nursing assistants, trained caregivers, and experienced activity coordinators who are passionate about serving others. Ongoing training ensures our staff stays current with best practices in adult care.

04

Safe & Welcoming Environment

Our facility is designed with safety and comfort in mind. From accessible spaces to secure premises, we provide an environment where participants can relax, engage, and feel at home.
